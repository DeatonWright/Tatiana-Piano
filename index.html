About Tatiana Wright

Tatiana Wright is a classical pianist and educator with a Masterâ€™s degree in Piano Performance and a broad range of musical interests. As a piano teacher, she is committed to helping students of all ages and abilities discover confidence, artistry, and joy in playing the instrument. With years of experience teaching in different settings, Tatiana uses an individualized approach that builds strong technique, expressive interpretation, and a lifelong appreciation for music. Her teaching style is supportive, patient, and motivating, focusing on guiding each student toward their personal goals, whether it is a formal study or learning for personal enrichment.

Besides her teaching duties, Tatiana is an accomplished performer and a collaborative pianist. She has worked extensively with instrumentalists, vocalists, chamber groups, choirs, and other kinds of small and large ensembles, providing sensitive and reliable accompaniment. She has participated in musical theater shows, opera productions, church services and holiday programs, as well as performing solo recitals and playing for student concerts, competitions, juries, auditions, and recordings.

Tatiana also offers professional services for a wide range of events, including weddings, celebration of life services, ceremonies, and private gatherings, providing beautiful and adaptable music tailored to each occasion. Whether performing classical repertoire, background music, or customized selections for a special moment, she is dedicated to bringing artistry, professionalism, and a warm presence to every occasion.

With a strong foundation as both a performer and educator, Tatiana is passionate about sharing the joy of music by helping her students thrive, supporting fellow musicians, and bringing meaningful musical experiences to the community. In her free time, Tatiana enjoys flying airplanes with her husband and daughter, as well as cooking, gardening, and taking walks in nature.
